Soluna Alpine

Brand Identity & Visual Direction

In this branding project, Soluna Alpine was born from the concept of a 24/7 ski resort.
Being open 24/7 allows guests to freely explore the resort at their own pace.
By utilizing a dynamic day-to-night color palette and sleek, modern typography, the brand captures the seamless transition between sun and moon.
